## This Isn't the End

<div className="card margin-vert--md">
  <div className="card__header">
    <h3>Future Plans</h3>
  </div>
  <div className="card__body">
    <p>Regarding the future direction of this project, I am not abandoning it. I have significant plans moving forward:</p>
    
    <ul className="margin-top--md">
      <li>
        <strong>Better Data:</strong> I'm investing in an <strong>IEC711 coupler</strong> and <strong>Dayton Audio impedance testing kits</strong>. My goal is to buy a wide array of drivers and manually test them myself. By tracking source impedance and noise accurately, I can provide you with FRD and ZMA files that you can just "drag and drop" into a simulator with 100% confidence
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>Custom Crossover Simulator:</strong> I'm aiming to build a custom crossover simulator better suited for creating IEM crossovers and overall acoustics - simulating inner-ear acoustics, better integration with FRD and ZMA files, simulation of ear seal leakage, and better integration with the data I'll be collecting
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>New Models:</strong> I want to explore TWS (True Wireless) designs and other hardware innovations once the "Reference" wired model is fully settled
      </li>
    </ul>
  </div>
